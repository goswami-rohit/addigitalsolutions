Questions, answered.

Do you only work with clients in the Northeast? +

The Northeast is our home and our focus, so you get genuine local presence and fast on-site response. That said, our cloud, hosting and software services can be delivered anywhere in India.

Can you really host Tally and Busy on the cloud? +

Yes. We host Tally Prime, Busy and virtually any Windows or web application on secure cloud servers, with multi-user access, daily backups and 24×7 monitoring. Your team works from any device, anywhere.

What does an AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) cover? +

AMC covers proactive monitoring, updates, security patching, backups and priority support for your servers, network, CCTV and systems — so issues are caught before they become downtime.

Do you handle both hardware and software? +

We do. From servers, networking, CCTV and IP-PBX hardware to custom software, websites and cloud — we integrate it all and stay your single point of accountability.

How do we get started? +

Send us a message or call. We'll understand your current setup, recommend the right approach honestly, and share a clear plan and price — no obligation.
